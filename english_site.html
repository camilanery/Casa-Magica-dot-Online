What are Simpatias?

Simpatia is a Brazilian superstition born out of mixing cultures, religions and traditions over many years. Today, it is used by many Brazilians without giving thought to what its origins are. It is simply something we do! Simpatia spells are cast using everyday objects that because of their symbolic qualities- become magical sources of power that help channel your wishes. The most important part of the Simpatia is believing very strongly in your desired outcome.

How to Cast a Spell:

1. Choose a Spell

2. Make your Wish

3. Watch the Ritual

The spell is cast for you digitally so there is no mess involved and chance of errors are minimized!
